COMMON QUESTIONS

What do I need to provide for mobile service? +
Just access to a water spigot/hose and an electrical outlet within reasonable distance of where the vehicle is parked. Shade is preferred but not required — we bring our own canopy when needed. We supply all equipment, products, and water filtration.
How long does a detail take? +
A Standard Detail runs 2.5–3.5 hours. A Premium takes 4–6 hours. Paint correction and ceramic coating may span two days. We'll give you an accurate estimate when you book based on your vehicle's size and condition.
How long does ceramic coating last? +
Our standard ceramic coating lasts 2 years with proper maintenance. We also offer premium 5-year coatings. Both provide UV protection that's critical for Florida's intense sun — the ceramic layer helps prevent paint fade and oxidation.
What products do you use? +
Professional-grade products from Gtechniq, CarPro, Gyeon, Koch Chemie, and Rupes. No off-the-shelf consumer products. Our ceramic coatings are certified professional-only formulations not available to the public.
What's your cancellation policy? +
We ask for 24-hour notice for cancellations or rescheduling at no charge. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a $50 trip fee. We're flexible with weather-related reschedules — it's Florida, we understand.
