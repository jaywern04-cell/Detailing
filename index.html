FAQ
COMMON QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know before booking your first detail.

What do I need to provide for mobile service? +
Just access to a water spigot/hose and an electrical outlet within reasonable distance of where the vehicle is parked. Shade is preferred but not required — we bring our own canopy when needed. We supply all equipment, products, and water filtration.
How long does a detail take? +
A Basic Wash takes about 1–1.5 hours, a Standard Detail runs 2.5–3.5 hours, and a Premium Detail typically takes 4–6 hours depending on vehicle size and condition. Paint correction or ceramic coating may require additional time. We'll give you an accurate estimate when you book.
How long does ceramic coating last? +
Our standard ceramic coating lasts 2 years with proper maintenance. We also offer premium 5-year coatings for clients who want maximum longevity. Both options provide incredible UV protection — critical for Florida's intense sun exposure.
What products do you use? +
We use professional-grade products from brands like Gtechniq, CarPro, Gyeon, Koch Chemie, and Rupes. No off-the-shelf consumer products. Our ceramic coatings are certified professional-only formulations that aren't available to the public.
What's your cancellation policy? +
We ask for 24-hour notice for cancellations or rescheduling at no charge. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a $50 trip fee. We're flexible with weather-related reschedules since — well, it's Florida.
